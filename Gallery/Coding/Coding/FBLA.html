Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) is an American career and technical student organization. It is a non-profit organization of high school and college students learning and traing to be leaders and have the leadership qualities.

Competive Events

FBLA has Competive Events in state and out of state. There are many different categories. There are Individual, Team, and Chapter events. There are also events specifically for high school students in grades 9-10. Specific events require certain things, such as a: demonstration, presentation, or a test. Click here to see all the FBLA competive events on their website.

FBLA Trips

FBLA hosts conferences that help students prepare for careers in business by focusing on academic competitions. FBLA conferences are a great place to network, develop your leadership skills, and visit exhibitors and vendors.
