Snow Activities

There are a lot of fun things to do in Flagstaff during the winter. You can build a snowman, make snow angels, go skiing or snowboarding, build a snow fortress, sheding, the possibilities are endless! Flagstaff has an average of 81 inches of snow a year.

Lowell Observation

Lowell Observatory is an independent, non-profit research institution founded in 1894. Which means it has been around for 125 years! You can learn a lot about astronomy in just a little bit of time. Click here if you want to buy tickets or donate money to support astronomy.
